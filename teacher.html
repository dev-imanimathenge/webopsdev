Welcome to my website

The modern classroom is undergoing a profound transformation as the seasoned intuition of the teacher meets the analytical precision of artificial intelligence. Rather than replacing the educator, AI serves as a powerful co-pilot, handling the heavy lifting of lesson planning and administrative grading so the teacher can focus on what a machine cannot: human connection. While an algorithm can personalize a math problem to a student's exact skill level, it is the teacher who recognizes the subtle frustration in a student's eyes or provides the mentorship that sparks lifelong curiosity. Together, they form a dynamic partnership where technology scales the reach of education, but the teacher remains the indispensable heart of the learning experience.
